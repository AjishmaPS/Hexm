Our Vision

Who we are

PAMA ( PATERNAL AND METERNAL ANCESTRY )

Institute for the Advancement of Transdisciplinary Archaeological Sciences (IATAS) is an independent,
non profit, public educational trust dedicated to fostering transdisciplinary knowledge. It is situated at
the archaeological site of Pattanam, an integral part of the renowned transoceanic port of Muziris, also
known asMuciri Pattinam, celebrated in Tamil, Sanskrit, Greek, and Latin classical literature.
